about us

ClosetGenius is an intuitive, smart wardrobe organization tool designed to simplify the way you plan your outfits. ClosetGenius eliminates the daily struggle of deciding what to wear, making it easier to get ready for any occasion. Whether you're facing a busy morning, packing for a trip, or just looking for a fresh combination from your existing wardrobe, ClosetGenius has got you covered.

With just a few clicks, our tool takes into account your wardrobe items and preferences to suggest a curated outfit for the day. Say goodbye to outfit fatigue and hello to a more organized, stress-free wardrobe!
